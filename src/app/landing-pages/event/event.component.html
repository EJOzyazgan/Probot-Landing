About Probot Playground AI Competition
Probot Playground Competition is a campus-wide coding event that is half hackathon and half career fair. The event will include a poker bot tournament and recruiting tables from AI companies. Attendees will consist of tournament participants, company recruiters, and students interested in AI.
Tournament Structure
The tournament will consist of 3-5 brackets. Each bracket will have three rounds respectively. Each round will have multiple ‘tables’ where 4-6 bots will compete. Each table will play up to three games with the top two bots moving on to the next round. The final and 3rd round will only have one winner who will move on to the final table. The final table will play up to five games and will only have one winner.
